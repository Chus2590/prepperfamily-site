🫙 Step-by-Step Guide to Canning Meat in Glass Jars

Learn to preserve food safely and efficiently

🛡️ Food Safety

Destroys bacteria and spores that cause botulism and other serious health risks.

💰 Save Money & Time

Buy ingredients on sale, cook once, and store ready-to-eat meals.

🏠 Emergency Readiness

Jars don’t rely on a freezer or electricity; just cool, dark shelves.

Key Principle

Meats are low-acid foods; only pressure canning reaches ≥ 240 °F (116 °C) required to sterilize them safely.
